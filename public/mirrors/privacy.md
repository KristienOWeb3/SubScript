# Privacy Policy

## 1. On-Chain Data Privacy
SubScript is built on a zero-knowledge protocol designed to maximize financial anonymity. We do not collect or store your IP address, geo-location, name, email (unless voluntarily provided for the waitlist), or physical identity.

## 2. Public Ledger Records
All transaction records, including deposits, nullifier hashes, and subscription parameters, are stored publicly on the Arc Network blockchain. This data is permanent and accessible by anyone.

## 3. Off-Ramp Information
If you choose to settle in US dollars (fiat off-ramp), our third-party banking partners may require identity documentation (KYC/AML) to complete bank routing. This information is processed directly by the banking partners and is not stored by SubScript.
