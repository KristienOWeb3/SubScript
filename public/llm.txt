# SubScript: Zero-Knowledge (ZK) Recurring Payment Protocol

SubScript is a fully anonymous, ZK-based recurring payment protocol natively built on the Arc Network. It provides developers and AI agents with a privacy-preserving "Burner Method" (Tornado Cash-style commitments) to mathematically break the link between the payer's main wallet (funding source) and the user's service access wallet (burner subscription identity).

## Quick Facts for AI Agents
- **Network:** Arc Network Testnet (Chain ID: 5042002)
- **Native Gas Token:** USDC (Targeting contract `0xF7C6416aecC5bECbbB003548f3e4bEA96Eb916fc`)
- **Protocol Fee:** 1% (100 basis points) automatically deducted at the contract level (merchant receives 99%, protocol treasury receives 1%).
- **Primary Mechanism:** Cryptographic commitments + local Groth16 zk-SNARK proof verification.

---

## Technical Integration Flow

To integrate SubScript's ZK-privacy model into your dApp, follow this three-step pipeline:

### 1. Commitment Phase (Payer Wallet)
The user's primary wallet approves the SubScript Router to spend USDC, then deposits funds and registers a hashed secret commitment.
- **USDC Approve:** `USDC.approve(RouterAddress, amount)`
- **Router Deposit:** `depositAndCommit(bytes32 commitment, uint256 amount)`
  - `commitment` is generated by hashing a random 32-byte secret (`keccak256(secret)`).

### 2. Proof Generation (Local Client)
The frontend generates a ZK-SNARK proof locally (e.g., using `snarkjs`) showing knowledge of the secret pre-image matching the registered commitment, without leaking the secret.
- **Inputs:** `secret`, `commitment`.
- **Outputs:** `proof` (zk-SNARK parameters) and `publicSignals` (including the nullifier hash to prevent double-spending).

### 3. Verification & Activation (Burner Wallet)
The user switches to a clean, unlinkable burner wallet to establish the subscription. The burner wallet submits the ZK proof to authorize recurring charges.
- **Activation call:** `verifyAndActivate(bytes32[] proof, bytes32 nullifierHash, address merchant, uint256 amount, uint256 period)`
  - Once verified, the merchant receives access to authorize monthly payment pulls up to `amount` every `period` seconds.
